Join Entropy Based DDoS Defense Scheme In SDN(JESS)

分为三个小步：

  • nominal stage:switch sends headers of all packets to controller,controller calculates joint entropies of each pair
  • preparatory stage:controller generate current pair profiles and calculates joint entropies,difference exceeds ,DDoS attack is detected,maximum difference is SuspiciousPair and send to switch
  • active mitigation stage:switch creates SC of SuspiciousPair and send to controller,controller calculates correspoinding score and generates scoretable ST,rule table with a designated ,if score of entry under forward, otherwise drop.
